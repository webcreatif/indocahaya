Payment Options

Flexibility on Payment Methods
  1. By Cash 100% Blocking Funds at Joint Account
    Buyer should issue conditioal Standing Instruction, that the funds will be released by bank against the aceptance letter that signed by all of party based on the agreed conditions.
  2. By Irrevocable Non Transferable Local L/C (SKBDN)
    Surat Kredit Berdokumen Dalam Negeri, at Sight FOB Barge or Mother Vesel, issued by Indonesia Prime Bank.
  3. By Irrevocable Non Transferable Usance L/C 60 days after BL date for FOB; as for CFR can be accepted if applicant is top 10 prime bank only
  4. By Telegraphic Transfer (T/T) and covered 100% by SBLC, especially for yearly contract.
  5. By Irrevocable Non Transferable L/C at Sight for FOB
